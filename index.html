Cherryton Villagers Songs

Cherryton

18-03-2020

Dear diary,

Today is a big day!
I got the opportunity to travel to this deserted island two days earlier than planned and ofcourse I took it!
I finished everything I had to do during the day before I left.
Even thought it was already evening before I finished, but that didn't stop me.

When I arrived, I was greeted by this Tanuki named Tom Nook and his two nephews Timmy and Tommy. I got to meet Paula and Kid Cat as well! One of the nephews gave me a tent to set up and when I was done, I helped Paula and Kid Cat with theirs!
Did I mentioned that the island is full of cherries?? I couldn't think of any other name for the island than 'Cherryton', everyone agreed with me and that is how this deserted island will go by the name 'Cherryton' from now on.

Before I went to sleep Tom Nook gave me a map of the whole island.

19-03-2020

Dear diary,

When I woke up I was a little bit dissoriented, I lowkey forgot that I was nog at home anymore.
But I wasn't sad when I realised out that I am on a deserted island. Well, I can't call it deserted anymore, since we are with sixth here!
I went outside and greeted everyone. Kid Cat gave me a nice new Safari hat as well! I decided to wear it immediatly.

Later the day I learned to make some handy tools! I can catch bugs, fish and I can even dig up some fossils!
Oh that reminds me! Tom Nook knows this friend names Blathers and he can analyse these fossils.
I also learned how to make an axe, so I can chop some wood. Unfortunately, the tools are breakable.

20-03-2020

Dear diary,

Today is the big day! Is what I would have said, if I didn't got the opportunity leave home two days ago.
To celebrate this day, I decides to get a haircut! My hair so shorter! I love it.
But what I don't love are these tarantulas! There are so many tarantulas at night!
I managed to catch one, I will give this one to Blathers... But when I left the museum, another tarantula decides to chase me! I never panicked this much in my whole life!
I ran across the whole island! The tarantula didn't gave up.. I tried to catch it, but when I went to grab my net, the tarantula got me...

I don't remember how I got here, but I woke up at the front of my house.
